## MsoPresetTextEffectShape枚举
表示艺术字的预设文字效果形状类型。

| STOP |停止|
| TRIANGLE_UP |三角向上|
| TRIANGLE_DOWN |三角形向下|
| CHEVRON_UP |雪佛龙|
| CHEVRON_DOWN |雪佛龙|
| RING_INSIDE |环内|
| RING_OUTSIDE |环外|
| ARCH_UP_CURVE |上拱曲线|
| ARCH_DOWN_CURVE |下拱曲线|
| CIRCLE_CURVE |圆形曲线|
| BUTTON_CURVE |按钮曲线|
| ARCH_UP_POUR |拱顶倒|
| ARCH_DOWN_POUR |拱形倾倒|
| CIRCLE_POUR |圆形倒|
| BUTTON_POUR |按钮倒|
| CURVE_UP |曲线向上|
| CURVE_DOWN |向下曲线|
| CAN_UP |CanUp|
| CAN_DOWN | CanDown|
| WAVE1 | Wave1|
| WAVE2 | Wave2|

| DOUBLE_WAVE2 |双波2|

| SLANT_DOWN |斜向下|

| CASCADE_DOWN |向下级联|
| MIXED |混合的|
