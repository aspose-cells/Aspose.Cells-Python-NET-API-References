## TickLabels类
表示与图表轴上的刻度线关联的刻度线标签。



TickLabels 类型公开了以下成员：

### 特性
|属性|描述|
| :- | :- |
| [font](/cells/python-net/zh/aspose.cells.charts/ticklabels/font) |返回一个 [TickLabels.font](/cells/python-net/zh/aspose.cells.charts/ticklabels#font) 对象，它表示指定的 TickLabels 对象的字体。|
| [auto_scale_font](/cells/python-net/zh/aspose.cells.charts/ticklabels/auto_scale_font) |如果对象中的文本在对象大小更改时更改字体大小，则为真。默认值是true。|
| [background_mode](/cells/python-net/zh/aspose.cells.charts/ticklabels/background_mode) |获取和设置背景的显示方式|
| [rotation_angle](/cells/python-net/zh/aspose.cells.charts/ticklabels/rotation_angle) |表示顺时针旋转文本的角度。|
| [is_automatic_rotation](/cells/python-net/zh/aspose.cells.charts/ticklabels/is_automatic_rotation) |表示旋转角度是否自动|
| [number_format](/cells/python-net/zh/aspose.cells.charts/ticklabels/number_format) |表示 TickLabels 对象的格式字符串。|
| [number](/cells/python-net/zh/aspose.cells.charts/ticklabels/number) |表示 TickLabels 对象的格式编号。|
| [number_format_linked](/cells/python-net/zh/aspose.cells.charts/ticklabels/number_format_linked) |如果数字格式链接到单元格则为真<br/>（这样当单元格中的数字格式发生变化时，标签中的数字格式也会发生变化）。|
| [display_number_format](/cells/python-net/zh/aspose.cells.charts/ticklabels/display_number_format) |获取和设置刻度标签的显示数字格式。|
| [offset](/cells/python-net/zh/aspose.cells.charts/ticklabels/offset) |获取和设置标签与轴的距离。|
| [text_direction](/cells/python-net/zh/aspose.cells.charts/ticklabels/text_direction) |表示文本阅读顺序。|
| [reading_order](/cells/python-net/zh/aspose.cells.charts/ticklabels/reading_order) |表示文本阅读顺序。|
| [direction_type](/cells/python-net/zh/aspose.cells.charts/ticklabels/direction_type) |获取和设置文本的方向。|
| [tick_label_items](/cells/python-net/zh/aspose.cells.charts/ticklabels/tick_label_items) |获取 TickLabel 的项目信息。|
