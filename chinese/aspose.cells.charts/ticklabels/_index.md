## TickLabels类
表示与图表轴上的刻度线关联的刻度线标签。



TickLabels 类型公开以下成员：

### 属性
|属性|描述|
| :- | :- |
| [font](/cells/python-net/zh/aspose.cells.charts/ticklabels/font) |返回一个 [`TickLabels.font`](/cells/python-net/zh/aspose.cells.charts/ticklabels#font) 对象，该对象代表指定 TickLabels 对象的字体。|
| [auto_scale_font](/cells/python-net/zh/aspose.cells.charts/ticklabels/auto_scale_font) |如果对象中的文本字体大小随对象大小变化而变化，则为 True。默认值为 True。|
| [background_mode](/cells/python-net/zh/aspose.cells.charts/ticklabels/background_mode) |获取和设置背景的显示模式|
| [rotation_angle](/cells/python-net/zh/aspose.cells.charts/ticklabels/rotation_angle) |表示文本顺时针旋转的角度。|
| [is_automatic_rotation](/cells/python-net/zh/aspose.cells.charts/ticklabels/is_automatic_rotation) |表示旋转角度是否自动|
| [number_format](/cells/python-net/zh/aspose.cells.charts/ticklabels/number_format) |表示 TickLabels 对象的格式字符串。|
| [number](/cells/python-net/zh/aspose.cells.charts/ticklabels/number) |表示 TickLabels 对象的格式数字。|
| [number_format_linked](/cells/python-net/zh/aspose.cells.charts/ticklabels/number_format_linked) |如果数字格式与单元格关联，则为 True<br/> （这样，当单元格中的数字格式发生变化时，标签中的数字格式也会发生变化）。|
| [display_number_format](/cells/python-net/zh/aspose.cells.charts/ticklabels/display_number_format) |获取并设置刻度标签的显示数字格式。|
| [offset](/cells/python-net/zh/aspose.cells.charts/ticklabels/offset) |获取或设置标签与分类轴的距离。<br/>仅适用于类别（x）轴。|
| [text_direction](/cells/python-net/zh/aspose.cells.charts/ticklabels/text_direction) |表示文本阅读顺序。|
| [reading_order](/cells/python-net/zh/aspose.cells.charts/ticklabels/reading_order) |表示文本阅读顺序。|
| [direction_type](/cells/python-net/zh/aspose.cells.charts/ticklabels/direction_type) |获取并设置文本的方向。|
| [tick_label_items](/cells/python-net/zh/aspose.cells.charts/ticklabels/tick_label_items) |获取轴的显示刻度标签。|
| [alignment_type](/cells/python-net/zh/aspose.cells.charts/ticklabels/alignment_type) |获取并设置轴上刻度标签的文本对齐方式。|
