##  PdfConverter klass

###  Metoder
| Metod| Beskrivning|
| :- | :- |
| [`process(, template_file, result_file)`](/cells/python-net/sv/aspose.cells.lowcode/pdfconverter/process/#str-str) | Konverterar given mallfil till pdf.|
