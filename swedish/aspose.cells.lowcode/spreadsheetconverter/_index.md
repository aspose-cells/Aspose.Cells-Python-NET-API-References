##  SpreadsheetConverter klass
Konverterare för konvertering mellan olika kalkylbladsfilformat, såsom xls, xlsx, xlsb, kalkylblad ml...



Typen SpreadsheetConverter avslöjar följande medlemmar:

###  Metoder
| Metod| Beskrivning|
| :- | :- |
| [`process(, template_file, result_file)`](/cells/python-net/sv/aspose.cells.lowcode/spreadsheetconverter/process/#str-str) | Konverterar en given mallfil mellan kalkylbladsfilformat.|
| [`process(, load_options, save_options)`](/cells/python-net/sv/aspose.cells.lowcode/spreadsheetconverter/process/#aspose.cells.lowcode.lowcodeloadoptions-aspose.cells.lowcode.lowcodesaveoptions) | Konverterar mellan olika kalkylbladsfilformat.|



###  Exempel

```python
from aspose.cells.lowcode import SpreadsheetConverter

SpreadsheetConverter.process("template.xlsx", "template.xlsb")

```
